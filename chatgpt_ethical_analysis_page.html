Academic Integrity Within Education

ChatGPT's ability to generate original-looking content raises concerns about plagiarism and the authenticity of student work, potentially hindering critical thinking, problem-solving, and writing skills development, and creating educational equity issues.

Use in Therapy/Mental Health Support

The use of ChatGPT in therapeutic contexts poses ethical questions, risks of misinterpretation and harm, and significant privacy concerns, challenging the nuances of human empathy and understanding in mental health support.

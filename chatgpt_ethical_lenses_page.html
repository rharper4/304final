Introduction Context Ethical Lenses Ethical Analysis Recommendations

Privacy Perspective

From a privacy standpoint, ChatGPT offers both advantages like personal control over searches and secure interactions, and disadvantages such as concerns over personal data storage and utilization, and the risk of data breaches.

Government Lens

Government regulations can ensure ethical development and usage of ChatGPT, balancing national security risks with privacy rights of citizens, and addressing new ethical concerns in AI technology.

Updating University Policies

Universities need to update their policies in line with AI advancements, focusing on autonomy, democratic participation, and transparent AI use.

Biden-Harris Administration's Approach

The Biden-Harris administration promotes responsible AI, centered on human needs and safety, with an AI Bill of Rights and Risk Management Framework.

European Union's AI Act

The EU's AI Act is the first comprehensive AI law, focusing on safe, transparent, and non-discriminatory AI, with special attention to generative AI like ChatGPT.
